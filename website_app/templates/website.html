Analyzing Sentiment Through Twitter : Lite

Overview

How can we analyze and visualize data in a way that is more understandable to humans? In this project, we explore this question by collecting, analyzing, and visualizing data from Twitter. Generate your own data by making a query or look at pre-analyzed data and view our graphical representation and comments about the dataset.
